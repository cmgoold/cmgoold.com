Welcome! This is my small slice of the internet to write about data science, statistics, software engineering, science, and a smattering of more informal topics too.

My name is Conor. I live in North East England, working remotely as a data scientist. I was inspired by the authenticity of the IndieWeb movement, and from reading many great personal blogs on platforms such as Bear Blog.
